# CleanMoto - Business Model (Text Format)

Key Partners

- Service Suppliers: Providers of cleaning chemicals, sanitizers, and consumable materials used in helmet cleaning operations.
- Equipment Providers: Sources of cleaning tools and maintenance equipment required for service quality and speed.
- Technology Providers: Hosting and platform support partners that keep the booking and notification system available.

Examples:
CleanMoto Helmet Cleaning Service

Key Activities

- Appointment Management: Handling online bookings, walk-in entries, and schedule coordination.
- Helmet Cleaning Operations: Performing service packages and add-on procedures based on customer selection.
- Customer Update Handling: Managing status updates and in-app notifications from booking to completion.

Examples:
CleanMoto Helmet Cleaning Service

Key Resources

- Service Workforce: Admin, staff, and front-desk personnel managing day-to-day operations.
- Digital Platform: Role-based booking, scanner, and notifications system built for operational control.
- Operational Data: Service catalog, appointment records, customer profiles, and activity logs.

Examples:
CleanMoto Helmet Cleaning Service

Value Proposition

- Clean and Sanitized Helmets: Professional helmet cleaning focused on hygiene and comfort.
- Convenient Service Access: Customers can book online or be assisted through walk-in scheduling.
- Transparent Service Flow: QR check-in and status notifications improve visibility and customer trust.

Examples:
CleanMoto Helmet Cleaning Service

Customer Relationships

- Assisted Service: Staff support for walk-ins, booking concerns, and appointment updates.
- Self-Service Access: Customers can manage appointments and profile information through their account.
- Ongoing Communication: Notification-driven updates keep customers informed throughout the service lifecycle.

Examples:
CleanMoto Helmet Cleaning Service

Channels

- Physical Service Point: On-site service handling for scheduled and walk-in customers.
- Web Platform: Online booking and account management via role-based pages.
- In-App Notification Channel: Header and notifications page for real-time status visibility.

Examples:
CleanMoto Helmet Cleaning Service

Customer Segments

- Helmet Owners and Commuters: Individuals needing regular cleaning and sanitation for daily use.
- Walk-In Customers: Nearby customers who prefer in-person scheduling and immediate assistance.
- Rider Communities: Groups and communities that can drive repeat usage and referrals.

Examples:
CleanMoto Helmet Cleaning Service

Cost Structure

- Operating Costs: Cleaning materials, utilities, and day-to-day consumables.
- Labor Costs: Staff salaries, training, and service delivery manpower.
- Technology Costs: Hosting, maintenance, and system support for the digital platform.

Examples:
CleanMoto Helmet Cleaning Service

Revenue Streams

- Service Package Sales: Main income from core helmet cleaning service packages.
- Add-On Sales: Extra revenue from optional add-on services selected during booking.
- Walk-In Service Sales: Revenue from appointments created directly at the branch.

CleanMoto Helmet Cleaning Service
