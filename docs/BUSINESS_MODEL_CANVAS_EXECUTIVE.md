# CleanMoto - Executive Business Model Canvas (One-Page)

## Investor Snapshot
CleanMoto is a role-based service operations platform for helmet cleaning businesses that combines online booking, walk-in intake, QR check-in, and real-time appointment notifications in one workflow.

## Problem
- Helmet cleaning shops lose revenue due to manual scheduling, missed walk-ins, and slow check-in.
- Customers lack visibility after booking, which hurts trust and repeat usage.
- Staff operations are fragmented across paper, chat, and ad hoc updates.

## Solution
- Unified booking for registered users plus staff-driven walk-in scheduling.
- QR-based appointment validation and check-in for faster front-desk flow.
- Role-based dashboards (admin, staff, user) with status and notification loops.
- Service and add-on catalog management to support upsell and pricing control.

## Target Customers
- Helmet owners and commuters who need regular helmet cleaning.
- Local walk-in customers near branch locations.
- Rider groups and communities.
- Future B2B fleets (delivery and courier riders).

## Value Proposition
- Faster intake and cleaner operations.
- Better customer experience with status transparency.
- Higher revenue per transaction through add-ons.
- Better capacity utilization through online + walk-in demand capture.

## Revenue Model
- Per-appointment service fees.
- Add-on upsell revenue.
- Walk-in transaction revenue.
- Expansion options: membership bundles and fleet contracts.

## Cost Structure
- Staff labor and operations.
- Cleaning supplies and equipment maintenance.
- Platform hosting, maintenance, and support.
- Security hardening and local marketing.

## Go-To-Market
- Branch-level rollout to prove operational efficiency.
- Partnerships with rider groups and local commuting communities.
- Promotions for first booking and repeat booking bundles.
- Referral loops via satisfied riders and community channels.

## Early Moat Signals
- Workflow integration across booking, check-in, and notifications.
- Role-based operational data that improves scheduling decisions.
- Local service relationships and repeat customer behavior.

## 12-Month Priorities
1. Strengthen payment workflow and confirmation trail.
2. Launch loyalty and repeat-booking mechanics.
3. Add KPI dashboard for conversion, completion, and average order value.
4. Pilot fleet/rider-group package pricing.

## Core KPIs For Investors
- Monthly bookings growth.
- Completion rate.
- Average order value.
- Repeat customer rate.
- Walk-in to booked conversion.
- Check-in to completion turnaround time.
