## How This Maps To The Current System

1. Online bookings and add-ons drive core and upsell revenue.
2. Walk-in calendar allows direct offline demand capture.
3. QR scanner flow improves intake speed and reduces manual errors.
4. Notifications improve service transparency and customer trust.
5. Admin service management enables offer and pricing optimization.

## Suggested KPIs For This Canvas

- Daily bookings (online vs walk-in)
- Completion rate per day/week
- Average transaction value (service plus add-ons)
- Repeat customer rate
- Cancellation and no-show rate
- Check-in to completion turnaround time
